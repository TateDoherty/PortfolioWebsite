Cover Letter

Tate Doherty

Growing up, my parents pursued quite different job trajectories. My mother is a talented professional artist while my father works as a mechanical engineer. My mother taught me to be more creative and to use my imagination to solve solutions whereas my father insisted on using mathematics and practicality. Through these distinct perspectives, I have incorporated the creativity and rationality that is presented when dealing with data in order to prepare myself for a position as a data analyst. I am eager to keep looking for innovative methods to display my love of numbers and data to identify new potential opportunities for growth and support organizations through effective data analysis. Several qualities to display my abilities are listed below:

  • Data Analysis: Being a podcast editor requires obtaining, analyzing, and evaluating data on views, subscriber count, and interactions in order to develop original podcast ideas and produce daily material. By creating daily content and offering effective input and feedback, I have increased the overall number of subscribers on my company's YouTube channel by 300 and the number of views to 50,000 in a year.
  • Technical Skills: As an Autonomous Aerospace Intern at Northrop Grumman, I participated in 10 technical meetings and presentations and developed technical skills in programming languages such as HTML and Python. My education in MIS at SDSU has further enhanced my technical skills in CSS, SQL, and Statistics through courses such as Web Development, Statistics, Data Management Systems, Object Oriented Programming, etc.
  • Organization Skills: As a Supervisor at High Country West Club, I was in charge of archiving over 5,000 appointments for visitors, which required careful attention to detail and adept data analytic skills to assure correctness and effectiveness. I also oversaw the pool's daily operations, such as upkeep, cleaning, and customer relations.

I appreciate your time to consider my application for the Data Analyst position. I am excited about the chance to discuss my qualifications with you in an interview. Please contact me by phone number or email if you have any further questions.

Sincerely,
Tate Doherty
